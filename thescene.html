The Scene

Publication Design

The Scene is a single-issue experiemental magazine. It was created as a part of a classroom project. The purpose of this magazine was to understand the modular grids within layouts. The magazine experiments with how text and image talk to each other in a layout and to some extent, how grids can change the way photographs are viewed. Throughout the issue, inconsistenct treatment remains key to keeping the character of each article alive. To read the entire magazine, go here.
