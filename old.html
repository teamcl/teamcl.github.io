The Stretch Cord

top
Red rope is hard and don’t have any elasticity. Using a red rope can help elders train their joint and pull their muscle to cure some body illness like scapulohumeral periarthritis.
Green rope has elasticity. Using a green rope can train elders’ strangth and muscle to keep their self-care ability in a good condition.

Different ropes can be plugged into the handle to achieve multiple functions.

Push the switch button to turn on the stretch cord.

When you are having exercise your stretch will give your data to the terminal.

Combining with your vital sign, the terminal will make a feasible training plan for you.
