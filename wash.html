Because the bathroom is use for rural area, I could no longer use the design elements of hi-technology or sci-fi. So how to define the form style of a rural area product was also a challenge to me. To me, the most successful part was by using resource of rural area and related techology, I created a bathroom which is suitable for the country-side and local elderly and thus embody the rural area topic.
